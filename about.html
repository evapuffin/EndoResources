Who we are

Our IT is responsible for a complex technical landscape encompassing approximately 5,000 users and more than 350 business applications in 14 different locations. To best serve you, we are organized in a way that promotes fit-for-purpose products and services to you and our valued business partners.
Our IT is fully committed to operational efficiency in all aspects of the business—and it shows in our customer interactions, applications, data and infrastructure management, service delivery and governance.

Specifically, Our IT is responsible for:

  • Business Partner Services
  • End-User Services
